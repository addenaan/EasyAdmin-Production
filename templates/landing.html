Designed around South African workforce compliance.

The system helps business users follow consistent rules for working time, leave, payroll finalisation and SARS PAYE calculations. Statutory outputs remain internal-use unless certified for official SARS filing specifications.

BCEA-focused workforce rules

Support operational discipline with configurable work patterns and rules aligned to common Basic Conditions of Employment Act requirements.

  • ✓ Contract types for 5-day, 6-day, contract and shift-worker schedules
  • ✓ Weekly hours grouped Monday to Sunday with warning indicators
  • ✓ Sunday and public-holiday premium pay logic by contract type
  • ✓ Annual leave accrual with 18-month expiry handling
  • ✓ Sick leave tracked in 36-month employee cycles

SARS PAYE calculation support

Payroll calculations use the target payroll date to determine the tax year and apply saved tax table logic.

  • ✓ Payroll blocks when the correct SARS tax year table is missing
  • ✓ PAYE age bands: under 65, 65–74 and 75+
  • ✓ Regular income annualisation and irregular income comparison method
  • ✓ UIF at 1% with contribution limit handling
  • ✓ EMP201, IRP5 and EMP501 draw from finalised ledger payslips only
