Bulk Finalize Payroll

This will calculate and save payslips to the Ledger for all selected employees. It does not email payslips or generate PDFs.

Process Bulk Payslips

This will generate and optionally email payslips for all selected employees. Finalised ledger payslips are used where available. If no ledger payslip exists, a Draft Payslip is generated and clearly marked. This does not save anything to the ledger.

Requires valid email addresses in profiles and configured SMTP settings.
Generate Bulk IRP5s

This will automatically compile and download individual IRP5 PDF documents for all selected employees based on their Ledger history.
